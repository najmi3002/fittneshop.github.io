There are some tips for beginner to start exercise

1. Set realistic goal.it may take some time to get the results you want. It’s vital to break your ultimate goal down in to stages and set lots of smaller goals to keep you motivated along the way.

2.Be consistent. Results come through regular and consistent activity. Stick to your programme and avoid frequent stops and starts.

3.Warm up.Not warming up is a mistake many beginners make. Before any session ensure that you warm up thoroughly and mobilise the areas that you will be using during the workout. Furthermore, failure to warm up properly may increase the risk of injury.

4.Will power.Don’t give up. All beginners have set backs on the way to achieving their goals. Accept them and use them to re-motivate yourself.
